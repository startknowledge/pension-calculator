How Army Pension Is Calculated in India (2025)

The Indian Army pension system for JCOs, ORs and Officers is governed by the Ministry of Defence (MoD) and implemented through PCDA (Pensions). Pension is calculated based on last drawn emoluments, qualifying service, and applicable Pay Commission rules.

Key Components of Pension Calculation

  • Qualifying service (minimum 20 years for full pension)
  • Last basic pay + MSP (where applicable)
  • Dearness Allowance (DA)
  • Commutation of pension
