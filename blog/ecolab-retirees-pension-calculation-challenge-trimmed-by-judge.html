The "trimmed" adjustments refer to reductions in your pension benefit that may apply due to:

Step-by-Step Pension Calculation Breakdown

  1. Service Credit Calculation

    • Your pension is based on years of service (creditable service).
    • Example: If you worked 25 years at Ecolab, your service credit is 25 years.

  2. Final Average Salary (FAS) Determination

    • Ecolab calculates your FAS using the highest 3–5 consecutive years of salary (depending on plan rules).
    • Example: If your highest-earning years were $80,000, $85,000, and $90,000, Ecolab may average these for your FAS.

  3. Pension Formula Application

    • The standard formula is: Monthly Benefit = (Service Credit × FAS × Benefit Multiplier)
    • For Ecolab, the benefit multiplier is typically 1.5% per year (e.g., 25 years × 1.5% = 37.5% of FAS).
    • Example: If your FAS is $90,000, your monthly benefit would be: $90,000 × 37.5% = $33,750 annually ($2,812.50/month)

  4. "Trimming" Adjustments

    • Early Retirement Reduction: If you retire before age 65, your benefit may be reduced by 1–5% per year.
    • Social Security Offset (WEP): If your Social Security benefit is low, Ecolab may reduce your pension to avoid "double-dipping."
    • COLA Caps: Some plans limit annual cost-of-living increases to 2–3%.
