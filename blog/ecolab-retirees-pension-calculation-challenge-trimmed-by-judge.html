2. How Ecolab’s Pension Formula Works

  • Typically calculated over the highest 3–5 consecutive years of employment (depending on the plan).
  • Example: If your highest-earning years were $80,000, $85,000, and $90,000, your FAS might be the average of these three years ($85,000).

B. Years of Service

  • Counts creditable years (full-time employment with Ecolab).

  • Example: A 30-year employee with 25 creditable years would use 25 in the calculation.

  • Ecolab’s multiplier is typically 1.5% to 2.0% per year of service, depending on the plan tier.
