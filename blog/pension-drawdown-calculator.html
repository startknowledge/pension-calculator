What is a Pension Drawdown Calculator?

A pension drawdown calculator is an online tool that allows individuals to calculate how much income they can expect to receive from their pension fund over a set period of time. It takes into account various factors, including the size of the pension fund, the individual's age, and their desired income level. By using a pension drawdown calculator, individuals can get a better understanding of their retirement finances and make informed decisions about how to access their pension funds.
