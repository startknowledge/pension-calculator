Understanding the Fitment Factor

The fitment factor is a crucial component of the 8th Pay Commission salary hike calculator. The fitment factor is used to calculate the new basic pay of an employee based on their current basic pay. A fitment factor of 2.10 means that the new basic pay will be 2.10 times the current basic pay. For example, if an employee's current basic pay is Rs. 50,000, their new basic pay will be Rs. 1,05,000 (50,000 x 2.10).
