The power of compound interest means even small increases can make a huge difference. For example:

4. Understand Your Pension Options Before Retirement

The AI chatbot will explain drawdown vs. annuities, but you can educate yourself now:

  • Annuity: Guaranteed income, but less flexibility.
  • Lump Sum: Tax-efficient, but runs out faster.

Pensions offer tax relief, but withdrawals are taxed. The AI chatbot will help optimize this, but you can start now:

  • Use the 25% tax-free lump sum (if applicable).
  • Spread withdrawals to stay in the lower tax bracket.
  • Consider a SIPP if you have high earnings (tax relief can be up to 40%).

Example: *A high-earning professional in the 40% tax bracket could get £40 tax relief for every £100 contributed—effectively £140 in savings for every £100 paid in.

The triple lock ensures State Pension increases, but private pensions may not keep up. The AI chatbot will adjust for inflation, but you can pre-plan:
