What are E-E-A-T Signals?

E-E-A-T is an acronym that stands for **Experience**, **Expertise**, **Authoritativeness**, and **Trustworthiness**. These signals are used by search engines to evaluate the credibility and reliability of a website, its content, and its authors. The concept of E-E-A-T was first introduced by Google in its Quality Rater Guidelines, which provide a framework for human evaluators to assess the quality of online content. By understanding and incorporating E-E-A-T signals into your digital strategy, you can significantly improve your website's visibility, drive more qualified leads, and ultimately, increase conversions.
